Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Get Started" button and follow the registration process.

Can I customize my tests?

Yes, Test Hub allows you to customize the questions, time limits, and scoring methods for your tests.

Can I reattempt my test?

Yes, Test Hub allows you to reattempt your test; there is no limit on attempts of a particular test.
